Workouts

Chest

Chest Flys

Description: Chest flys are an isolation exercise that primarily target the chest muscles. They are typically performed using dumbbells or a chest fly machine.

Goals:

Bodybuilding: Aim for 3 sets of 8-12 repetitions with 1-2 minutes of rest between sets.

Strength (Powerlifting): Not typically used in powerlifting routines.

High Volume Training: Aim for 4 sets of 15-20 repetitions with short rest periods of 30-60 seconds between sets.

Bench Press

Description: Bench press is a compound exercise that primarily targets the chest muscles.

Goals:

Bodybuilding: Aim for 3 sets of 8-12 repetitions with 1-2 minutes of rest between sets.

Strength (Powerlifting): Aim for 5 sets of 5 repetitions with 3-5 minutes of rest between sets, using heavier weights.

High Volume Training: Aim for 4 sets of 15-20 repetitions with short rest periods of 30-60 seconds between sets.

Leg Press

Bodybuilding: Aim for 3 sets of 8-12 repetitions with 1-2 minutes of rest between sets.
