Breakfast: Scrambled Tofu with Spinach and Tomatoes

Ingredients:

  • 200g firm tofu, crumbled
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add crumbled tofu. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add chopped spinach and diced tomato to the skillet. Cook until the spinach is wilted and the tomatoes are softened.
  3. Season with turmeric powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy a protein-packed breakfast!

Lunch: Grilled Chicken Salad with Avocado

Ingredients:

  • 150g grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed greens (spinach, lettuce, arugula)
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Arrange mixed greens on a plate and top with grilled chicken breast slices, avocado, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber.
  2. Drizzle balsamic vinegar and olive oil over the salad.
  3. Toss gently to coat everything evenly.
  4. Serve fresh and enjoy a light yet satisfying lunch!

Dinner: Baked Salmon with Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • 150g salmon fillet
  • 1/2 lemon, sliced
  • 100g asparagus spears
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place salmon fillet on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Arrange lemon slices on top of the salmon.
  3. Trim the woody ends of the asparagus spears and place them around the salmon on the baking sheet.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the salmon and asparagus. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Serve hot with a side of steamed brown rice or quinoa, if desired.
