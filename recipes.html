Breakfast: Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado, mashed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast the slices of bread until golden brown.
  2. While the bread is toasting, bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer. Add the vinegar.
  3. Crack one egg into a small bowl. Using a spoon, create a gentle whirlpool in the simmering water and slide the egg into the center. Repeat with the second egg.
  4. Let the eggs poach for about 3-4 minutes for a runny yolk or longer for a firmer yolk.
  5. Remove the poached eggs with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel to drain.
  6. Spread mashed avocado evenly onto the toasted bread slices.
  7. Top each slice with a poached egg and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy a nutritious and delicious breakfast!

Macronutrient Breakdown (per serving):

  • Protein: ~15 grams
  • Carbohydrates: ~30 grams
  • Fat: ~20 grams
  • Calories: ~350

Lunch: Protein-Packed Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (or dairy-free cheese for a vegan option)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (or vegan cheese)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425째F (220째C).
  2. Place the whole wheat pizza crust on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread marinara sauce evenly over the pizza crust.
  4. Evenly distribute the shredded chicken breast, diced bell peppers, diced onions, and sliced black olives over the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the toppings.
  6. Season with Italian seasoning and crushed red pepper flakes if desired.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your protein-packed pizza!

Macronutrient Breakdown (per serving):

  • Protein: ~25-30 grams
  • Carbohydrates: ~30-40 grams
  • Fat: ~10-15 grams
  • Calories: ~350-400

Dinner: Grilled Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 150g grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, onions)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper, then grill for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  3. Toss the mixed vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then spread them on a baking sheet.
  4. Roast the vegetables in the oven at 400째F (200째C) for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and slightly charred.
  5. Serve the grilled chicken with the roasted vegetables and enjoy a nutritious dinner!

  • Protein: ~25-30 grams
  • Carbohydrates: ~10-15 grams
  • Fat: ~5-10 grams
  • Calories: ~250-350
