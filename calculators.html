About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It is commonly used to assess whether someone has a healthy body weight for their height. BMI is calculated by dividing weight in kilograms by the square of height in meters (BMI = weight / (height * height)).

Healthy BMI ranges:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 - 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 - 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or greater
