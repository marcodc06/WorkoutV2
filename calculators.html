About Calorie Calculation

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is the estimated number of calories you burn in a day. It includes calories burned through activities, exercise, and basic bodily functions.

Choosing the right activity level is crucial for accurate calorie estimation. Be honest about your activity level to get the best results.

The calorie estimates provided below can help you maintain, gain, or lose weight based on your goals and activity level.

BMI Calculator





About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It is commonly used to assess whether someone has a healthy body weight for their height.

The BMI results can help you determine if you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese based on established BMI ranges.
